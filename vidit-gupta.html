Leader. Bowler. Part-time WicketKeeper. Division Champion. Heart of the 2nd XI.

Vidit Gupta

🏏 2nd XI Captain ❄️ Captain Cool 🏆 Division 12 Champion

From the cricket-mad streets of India to the heart of Brighton Dome Mission CC, Vidit Gupta has spent years quietly building something special — a team that wins together, grows together, and celebrates together. Calm under pressure, fierce with the ball, and always leading by example.

Where It All Began

"I am originally from India, where cricket is more than just a sport — it's a passion shared by millions. My cricket journey began at a young age, playing with friends and enjoying the game purely for fun.

Over the years, that love for cricket has only grown stronger, and it continues to be a big part of my life."

The Captain's Journey

"Captaining the 2nd XI over the past few years has been a rewarding experience. It has taught me the importance of communication, patience, and leading by example.

I've grown both as a cricketer and as a leader, learning how to bring the best out of players with different strengths and personalities while building a positive team culture."
