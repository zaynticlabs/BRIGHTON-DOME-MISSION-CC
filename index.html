    1937 — Dome Mission Founded

    Established in 1937, Dome Mission began its journey as a club committed to bringing people together through cricket in the Brighton area.

    1990s-2000s — Decades of Community Cricket

    Through the decades, Dome Mission built a reputation as one of Brighton's most welcoming and inclusive cricket clubs, attracting players from across England and India.

    2016 — 🏆 WSIL Division 6 Champions

    Brighton Dome Mission win the West Sussex Invitation League Division 6 title — the club's first major honour in the modern era.

    2017 — 🏆 WSIL Division 5 Champions — Back to Back!

    Successive league title wins cement Dome Mission's reputation as one of the most competitive clubs in West Sussex.

    2018 — 🏆 Sussex League Division 7C Winners

    The club transitions to the Sussex Cricket League and immediately makes its mark — Division 7C Champions.

    2019 — 🏆 Sussex League Division 6C Winners

    Another league title — three in four years. Dome Mission's golden era of cricket continues.

    2021 — 🥈 Runners Up — PROMOTED

    Despite finishing runners-up, Dome Mission earn promotion to a higher division. Also runners-up in the Sussex Slam T20 competition and semi-finalists in the Trophy.

    2026 — 1st XI in Division 4 — Higher Than Ever

    Dome Mission's 1st XI compete in Division 4 East — the highest level in the club's history. Up the Dome! 🏏
